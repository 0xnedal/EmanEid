About Me

I am an artist specializing in a unique form of art called Geometric Art.
Geometric Art is characterized by intricate shapes, patterns, and symmetry, which are the foundation of my creative expression.
My artwork often combines mathematical precision with artistic aesthetics, resulting in visually captivating and thought-provoking pieces.
Geometric Art allows me to explore the beauty of symmetry and precision while using various mediums to bring my ideas to life. I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of traditional art and constantly seeking new ways to blend mathematics and creativity in my work.
